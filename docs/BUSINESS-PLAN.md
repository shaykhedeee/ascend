# ASCEND - Comprehensive Business Plan & Launch Strategy

## Executive Summary

ASCEND is an AI-powered habit tracking and goal decomposition web application that transforms ambitious life goals into achievable daily tasks. Built as a Progressive Web App (PWA), it works across all devices with a native app experience.

**Value Proposition:** We help users become their ideal selves by using AI to break down overwhelming goals into manageable daily habits, gamifying the transformation journey.

## 5. Conversion Optimization

### Free â†’ Pro Conversion Triggers

   - After 7-day streak: "Protect your progress with Streak Freeze"
   - After 21-day streak: "You've built a habit! Unlock advanced analytics"

   - "Join 500+ Pro members transforming their lives"

### Best Practices
- Never interrupt task completion
- Always provide "Maybe Later" option
- Show value before asking for money
- Celebrate achievements, then suggest upgrade
