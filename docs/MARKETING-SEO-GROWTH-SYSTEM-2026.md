# Resurgo Marketing + SEO Growth System (2026)

## Core shifts shaping the strategy

This strategy is built around three 2026 realities:

1. **Search is now answer-first, not click-first.**
   - Google and HubSpot both emphasize answer-engine optimization (AEO): direct answers, structured content, clear schema, and citations matter more than raw blue-link rankings.
2. **People want immediate progress, not distant promises.**
   - Google trend guidance for 2026 shows buyers increasingly respond to tangible, present-tense wins. Resurgo should sell *clarity today* and *momentum this week*, not just transformation someday.
3. **Brand trust is built across channels, not only on-site.**
   - HubSpot’s loop-marketing model reinforces the need to express, tailor, amplify, and evolve. Content should become a cross-channel signal system: blog, guides, product pages, social snippets, email, community, and AI-visible answers.

## Distribution loop

### Express
Document the brand voice as:
- direct
- sharp
- practical
- anti-fluff
- slightly pixel-terminal, but still readable

### Tailor
Personalize CTAs by page type:
- awareness article -> read next / try free
- comparison article -> see pricing / try free
- template article -> load template / sign up

### Amplify
Every major blog should spin into:
- 3 short-form posts
- 1 carousel/thread
- 1 email snippet
- 1 Reddit/community-style answer draft
- 1 FAQ addition on-site

### Evolve
Weekly review metrics:
- top pages by sign-up assists
- blog posts with strongest CTA clicks
- high-impression / low-click pages to rewrite
- question clusters not yet answered on-site
- branded search growth and direct traffic lift

## Metrics that matter most

Stop over-weighting only pageviews. Track:
- sign-up assists from content
- pricing clicks from content
- internal-link depth
- article-to-article continuation rate
- branded search growth
- manual AI visibility checks for core questions

- repurpose every strong page across channels

Resurgo should position itself as the **AI habit tracker and life operating system that turns clarity into execution**.
